Who is Rick Sear?


Rick is currently studing Physics and Computer Science at the George Washington University. He hails from Wisconsin and is interested in artificial intelligence, nuclear physics, and philosophy (among other things).

He is currently working with Dr. Neil Johnson’s research team studying the many-body physics of online extremist groups. He’s also the President of GW Robotics, the Layout Manager of the GW Undergraduate Review, and is involved with GW’s chapter of the ACM.

Rick has taken or is currently taking the following notable courses:

  • Data Structures
  • Computer Architecture
  • Continuous Algorithms (in progress)
  • Multivariable Calculus
  • Differential Equations (ordinary and partial -- in progress)
  • Modern Physics
  • Software Engineering
  • Databases (in progress)
